🏛 Ontario Trillium Foundation

OTF Seed Grant

Ontario Trillium Foundation · annual cycle

Opens Jul 22 · Deadline Aug 19, 2026
Amount: $10,000 – $75,000 Term: 6 or 12 months Decisions: ~4–5 months after deadline

Application checklist

  • OTF Granting Portal account created at otf.ca
  • Organization registered/incorporated 12+ months
  • Deliver programs in one of OTF's 16 catchment areas
  • One of 4 eligible sectors confirmed
  • Project description — new pilot or capacity building
  • Detailed budget with quotes for items over $5,000
  • Audited financials or CRA T3010 (most recent year)
  • Financial statements demonstrating org health
  • Business Number on file
  • Letters of community support (if collaborative)

Covers: Staffing/salaries for project delivery, program costs, materials, digital capacity, org planning · advance payments available · first-timer friendly

OTF Grow Grant

Ontario Trillium Foundation · annual cycle

Opens Oct 7 · Deadline Nov 4, 2026
Amount: $100,000 – $600,000 Term: 2 or 3 years Decisions: ~4–5 months after deadline

Application checklist

  • OTF Granting Portal account at otf.ca
  • Organization registered/incorporated 12+ months
  • Proven program with demonstrated track record and data
  • Proof of ownership or 5-year lease agreement for space
  • Scaling or expansion plan with clear outcomes
  • Multi-year detailed budget (salaries eligible)
  • Audited financials (2+ years preferred)
  • Business Number on file
  • Collaborative Agreement (if joint application)
  • Quotes/estimates for goods/services over $5,000

Covers: Program salaries, scaling and delivery costs, reaching new populations · largest OTF grant — best for established orgs with multi-year program data

🍁 Federal — Open to Any Province

New Horizons for Seniors Program (NHSP)

Employment & Social Development Canada · 2026–27 cycle

Call launching imminently
Amount: Up to $50,000 per project Term: Up to 12 months Pool: $54M+ nationally

Application checklist

  • GCOS account created at canada.ca
  • Project designed by and for seniors (55+)
  • Seniors in lead, planning, or delivery roles
  • Meets at least 1 of 5 NHSP objectives
  • Primary and secondary contact identified
  • Detailed project budget (equipment and fees eligible)
  • Letters of community/partner support
  • Project start date after funding agreement signed

Covers: Equipment, program delivery, professional fees, capital for senior-focused programs · subscribe at canada.ca/nhsp — call could open any day

SDPP — Children & Families / Disability

Employment & Social Development Canada · ad hoc calls

Watch — could open anytime
SDPP-CF: Varies · multi-year contributions SDPP-D: Up to $150,000 over 3 years

Preparation checklist (prepare now, apply when call opens)

  • Alert set at canada.ca/en/esdc/funding
  • CRA charitable registration confirmed
  • Program description and target population
  • Staff and wage cost breakdown ready
  • Partnership letters drafted (if applicable)
  • Multi-year evaluation framework outlined

Covers: Wages and employment-related costs explicitly eligible · children, families, disability, social inclusion

Law Foundation of Ontario — Responsive Grants

Law Foundation of Ontario

No deadline · rolling
Amount: Up to $250,000 over 2 years Apply: lawfoundation.on.ca

Application checklist

  • Non-profit, charity, or Indigenous group status
  • Project focused on access to justice in Ontario
  • Program description and underserved population identified
  • Budget including staff costs (eligible)
  • Organizational financials
  • Evaluation and reporting plan

Covers: Program staffing costs, legal education and research delivery · one of Ontario's largest rolling grant opportunities

George Lunan Foundation

Private foundation · Ontario only

No deadline · rolling
Amount: Typically up to $5,000

Application checklist

  • Ontario-based registered non-profit or charity
  • Project in arts, education, environment, health/welfare, or social services
  • Brief org overview and mission statement
  • Specific funding request with budget

Covers: Operating funds, equipment, seed money, special projects · explicitly eligible · good for small or newer organizations
