Crime Prevention & Community Safety Fund

City of Calgary · CPIP + CSIF combined

Full apps due Jul 17
Amount: No min/max · proportionate to program Term: 3 years (Jan 2027 – Dec 2029) Pool: $9.5M/year

Application checklist

  • Invitation letter from EOI stage on file
  • Program description and theory of change
  • Detailed budget with salary breakdown
  • Evidence of community need / prior data
  • CRA charity registration or qualified donee status
  • Organizational financials (last 2 years)
  • FIMS account set up at calgary.ca
  • Letters of partner/community support

Covers: Program salaries, direct service delivery costs, operations · Note: invited EOI applicants only

CIP Project Grant

Government of Alberta

Deadline Aug 15, 2026
Amount: Up to 50% matching of eligible costs Portal opens: June 2026 via GATE Front Office

Application checklist

  • GATE Front Office account created
  • Alberta non-profit incorporation docs
  • Project description and outcomes
  • Detailed budget (wages, materials, program costs)
  • Proof of matching/community fundraising
  • Financial statements (most recent year)
  • No overdue reporting on prior AB grants
  • Project timeline (must complete within 18 months)

Covers: Wages, office space, materials, program delivery, community events, gender equity & 2SLGBTQQIA+ initiatives

Calgary Foundation — Community Grants

Calgary Foundation · Fall 2026 cycle

Opens late July · closes early Sep

🍁 Federal — open to any province

New Horizons for Seniors Program (NHSP)

Employment & Social Development Canada · 2026–27 cycle

Call launching imminently
Amount: Up to $50,000 per project Term: Up to 12 months Pool: $54M+ nationally

Application checklist

  • GCOS account at canada.ca (apply online)
  • Project designed by and for seniors (55+)
  • Seniors in lead/planning/delivery roles
  • Meets at least 1 of 5 NHSP objectives
  • Primary + secondary contact identified
  • Detailed budget (equipment, professional fees eligible)
  • Letters of community/partner support
  • Project start date planned for after agreement signing

Covers: Equipment, program delivery, professional fees, capital for senior programs · subscribe at canada.ca/nhsp — call could open any day
